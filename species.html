LIST OF POTENTIAL HOST SPECIES

In the Earth HoloGenome Initiative, we will prioritise the generation of hologenomic  data from phylogenetically closely related species pairs or complexes to address multiple biological issues by comparing intraspecific variation and interspecific variation between closely and more distantly related species.
